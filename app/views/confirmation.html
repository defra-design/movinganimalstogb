What happens next

You will need your reference number again, so keep it safe. We will ask for your reference number when you:

  • enter the border control post
  • contact us

If you have not added the CHED and VRN, add it later

We need these details at least 24 hours before your arrival. We will email you 24 hours before your arrival time reminding you to add these details.

We will use the VRN to recognise your vehicle and allow it into the border control post. We will use the CHED number to connect your inspection with the rest of your import documents.

Change or cancel your booking

You can change your arrival date or time anytime. You can also cancel your booking and start again.
