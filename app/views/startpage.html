Book a live animal inspection

You need to book an inspection if you are importing live animals into Great Britain. Inspections take place at these border control posts:

  • Sevington, England
  • Fishguard, Wales
  • Pembroke Dock, Wales
  • Holyhead, Wales
! Warning You must book a separate inspection for each vehicle you are transporting.

To book an inspection, you need to know the:

  • Type of vehicle you will use
  • Animal species
  • Number of animals
  • Estimated date of arrival
